No Limits: The Will to Succeed

No Limits goes behind the scenes to explore the hard work, sacrifice, and dedication Phelps had to endure throughout his training phases leading up to the 2004, 2008, 2012, and 2016 Olympic Games.

Phelps shares stories about family, his coach, and his passion for the sport. He highlights memorable races and lessons he has learned throughout the sport. Michaels success is all about his mindset. His coach, Bob Bowman says, Phelps has made a habit out of things other people aren't willing to do.

Whiplash

A young man named Andrew Neyman (Miles Teller) is practicing late at night at his music school in NY, one of the best in the country. When his drumming catches the ear of Mr. Fletcher, as Fletcher pauses, he listens, barks a few orders at the young man, and moves on, unsatisfied with what he heard.

Throughout the movie, Andrew transforms from an average percussionist - into a top jazz assemble. But Andrew's passion to achieve perfection quickly spirals into obsession. Throughout the movie, Fletcher pushes him to the brink of his ability and his sanity.
